About

Hi I'm Matt! I'm from the San Francisco Bay Area. I’m a software developer, entrepreneur, photographer, filmmaker and musician. While it seems like a lot, all these things can be summed up under one idea. Create stuff. The titles are just mediums that I use to build and make things.

On my free time, I enjoy getting out and exploring. If its not raining, you can find me hiking around the many trails in Vancouver, exploring unique areas of the city and playing soccer(football). I’m also training to run a half marathon sometime soon.
