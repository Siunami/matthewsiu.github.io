News Feedr

News Feedr is a Facebook messenger chat bot that aggregates news articles from popular news sources like Bloomberg, TechCrunch, NY Times, etc. and presents it to the user as a concise blurb right into your messenger chat. It lets you choose different topics like business, technology, economics and gathers news articles from all these sources.
