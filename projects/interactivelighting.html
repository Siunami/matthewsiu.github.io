Interactive Lights

Currently: Using an accelerometer, I can recognize when someone taps or hits the light. The light flashes with an intensity equivalent to the amount of force applied

Future: I am working with my professor to connect 8 of the lights in a row. Each one will not only light up when hit but also play a unique musical note. The row of 8 will act as one C Major octave.

Why

I am building this project with the goal of inspiring young people to get excited about the possibilites with technology. Technology is powerful. Arts can inspire. Blending both can bring new perspective and attention to technical fields. When finished, I plan on displaying the project in a public space.
