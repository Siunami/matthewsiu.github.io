About

Hi I'm Matt! I'm from the San Francisco Bay Area. I’m a software developer, entrepreneur, photographer, designer, filmmaker, writer and musician. While it seems like a lot, all these things can be summed up under one idea. Create stuff. The titles are just mediums that I use to build and make things.

Looking for something to match my varied interests, I decided to pursue a degree in Cognitive Systems at the University of British Columbia (Expected 2019). The courses cover a wide range of topics such as computer science, psychology, philosophy, linguistics and anthropology.

On my free time, I enjoy getting out and exploring. If its not raining, you can find me hiking around the many trails in Vancouver, exploring unique areas of the city and playing soccer(football). I’m also training to run a half marathon sometime soon.
